Causa

Description

It is one of the most iconic dishes of Peruvian cuisine. It consists of a type of mashed potato pie filled with chicken or tuna salad, along with other vegetables.

Here is the recipe Causa limena

Ingredients

For the mass:

  • 5 medium yellow potatoes
  • 4 yellow peppers
  • 4 lemons
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste.

For the filling:

  • 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 200 grams of chicken breast
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 boiled egg

Steps

For the mass:

  1. Steam the yellow potatoes for 30 minutes. Then remove them, peel them carefully, press them and let them cool.
  2. In a separate pot, steam the chicken breast. Once cooked, let it cool and chop it into strips.

For the filling:

  1. Chop the onion into squares, wash it and squeeze it until it is dry.
  2. Mix the onion with the lettuce pieces and mayonnaise.
  3. Mix the onion with the lettuce pieces and mayonnaise.
  4. Assemble the cause by making layers of dough, placing the filling and the avocado on top. Decorate with boiled egg, parsley and mayonnaise.
