Siu Mais

Description

They consist of small bags of wonton dough filled with different ingredients, usually pork, prawns, mushrooms, bamboo, Chinese rice wine (Shaoxing), sesame oil, etc. There are many versions of siu mai, and depending on the version certain ingredients and combinations will be used.

Recipe credits: Kwan Homsai Here is the recipe: Siu Mai

Ingredients

  • 20 units of wonton pasta
  • 200g minced pork
  • 2 shitake mushrooms brainy and hydrated
  • 5g chives (the green part)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1 pinch of white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon dark soy sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, place the prawns, half the cornstarch and salt. Mix in the same direction until you get a sticky paste. Cover the bowl for later.
  2. In a larger bowl, place the pork, the other half of the cornstarch, and the salt. Start removing it.
  3. Add the light soy sauce, the oyster sauce, the sugar and the white pepper. Remove for five minutes.
  4. Add the prawns, shitake mushrooms, spring onion, ginger and sesame oil. Stir for another 10 minutes until you get a sticky dough. Cover and leave in the fridge for 1 hour.
  5. Cut off the corners of the wonton dough.
  6. Using a knife, place 25g of the filling in the center of the wonton wrapper. Close the pasta forming a small open bag. Repeat the process until all the pieces are finished.
  7. In a bamboo basket or other steamer container, place perforated parchment paper on the bottom and the pieces on top.
  8. Steam the pieces over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes.

For the sauce:

  1. In a small skillet or pot, over medium heat, add the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, vinegar, and sugar.
  2. Stir well. When it starts to boil, turn off the heat and let cool. The sauce is ready to accompany the Siu Mais.
