Ceviche

Description

It is a fresh dish based mainly on fish, although it also includes other seafood. It is typical of Peru and is prepared practically throughout South America, at least in the part that faces the Pacific Ocean.

Here is the recipe: Ceviche

Ingredients

  • 800g fish fillet
  • 1 red onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 chili pepper minced
  • 1 tbsp. chopped coriander
  • Juice of 6 lemons
  • 1/2 tsp. of garlic
  • Pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 boiled sweet potato
  • 1 shelled corn
  • fresh lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Wash the fish. Cut it into cubes 1 to 2cm on each side. Season with salt. Save it for later.
  2. In a bowl, place the fish cubes and add the freshly squeezed lemon juice. The latter should cover the entire fish.
